Our Story

Over 70 years of building with integrity, quality, and a commitment to the Pacific Northwest.

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leader

Founded in 1947 by Gary Merlino, our company started with a single truck and a powerful vision: to provide exceptional construction services built on a foundation of hard work and integrity. Through the decades, we've grown alongside the city of Seattle, contributing to some of the region's most significant infrastructure projects.

Today, Gary Merlino Construction Co. is a third-generation family-owned business, but our founder's core values remain. We combine decades of experience with modern technology and a relentless pursuit of excellence to tackle complex projects and deliver unparalleled results for our clients.

More Than Just Construction

Our commitment extends beyond the job site. We believe in building stronger communities and ensuring the absolute safety of our employees, partners, and the public. Our "Safety First" culture is ingrained in every task we undertake, supported by continuous training and a proactive approach to risk management.

We are also dedicated to giving back to the community that has supported us for decades, participating in local initiatives and supporting charitable organizations across the Seattle area.
